Dara Varam

I'm a Master's student in Machine Learning at the American University of Sharjah, and I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering with a minor in Engineering Management.

My interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, machine learning, and robotics. Currently, I focus on model compression and optimization for deploying neural networks on resource-constrained edge devices. My expertise includes quantization techniques like integer and mixed-precision quantization, entropy coding, and privacy-preserving methods in edge computing. Additionally, I explore energy-efficient computing and intelligent autonomous robotics.
